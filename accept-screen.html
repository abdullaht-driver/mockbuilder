Market types

UFP vs Non-UFP markets

The accept screen behaves differently depending on whether the market uses Upfront Pricing.

UFP Market

Upfront pricing

313 markets

Drivers see the full fare before accepting. The card shows the exact dollar amount, bonus breakdown, and estimated hourly rate.

  • Full fare displayed (e.g. $20.85)
  • Bonus amount shown in green
  • Estimated hourly rate visible
  • Pickup and dropoff addresses shown
  • Most US markets operate in UFP
Non-UFP Market

Non-upfront pricing

15 markets

Drivers see limited fare info — typically just the estimated time and distance. Final fare is calculated after the ride completes.

  • No fare amount pre-ride
  • Distance and time estimates shown
  • Dropoff location may be hidden
  • Rate per mile/minute visible in some markets
  • Drivers are paid per minute and per mile based on city rates
